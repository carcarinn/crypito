Tips and Tricks

01

Buying Crypto Profiles

Verified accounts, country of origin and clear transaction history are things to look for when buying these profiles. It is best to avoid using your real IDs when buying, unless it is a credible source.

02

Selling Crypto Profiles

Selling crypto profiles needs preparation, ensuring the profiles are verified and appear active. State clear description and information to the buyers, to avoid suspicious activity.

03

Managing Crypto Profiles

When managing multiple profiles, use different and unique passwords with two verification steps. Avoid using the same device to reduce the risk of detection.
