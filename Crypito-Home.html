What is a Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a form of digital currency that do not operate under a central bank. Instead of using traditional financial institutions, it uses a blockchain technology, a distributed ledger system that tracks trades and purchases assets. Cryptocurrencies are used for a variety of purposes, starting from online purchases to investment. Their value depend on the demand, the more popular it is, the higher the value goes.
